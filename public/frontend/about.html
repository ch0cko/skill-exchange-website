ABOUT

Skill Circle is a community-powered platform where people teach what they know and learn what they love—no money involved. From hobbies to career skills, we make it easy to connect with others in your area and exchange knowledge in a fun, flexible way.

Why pay for lessons when you can swap skills and grow together? Whether you're learning for fun or self-improvement, Skill Circle is your space to share, connect, and evolve—one skill at a time.

FEATURES

Levelling system

Users can level up based on their participation, skill contribution and successful exchanges.

Reward system

A reward system where users can exchange credits for premium features and exciting rewards.

Skill Quests

Users can accept and create quests for exchanging and testing various skills.

Progress tracker

Users can visually track their learning progress with stats that show how many skills they've mastered, etc.

Content Verification

Resources posted by users will be verified by an admin for accuracy.

Leaderboards

A competitive element where users can see how they rank against each other.
